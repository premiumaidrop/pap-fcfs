Frequently Asked Questions

What is PAP Premium Airdrop?

PAP Premium Airdrop ($PAP) is an exclusive FCFS (First Come First Serve) waitlist rewards program designed to reward the first 1,000 early community supporters with a share of 20,000,000 PAP tokens (2% of total supply).

How do I qualify for the waitlist?

To qualify, you must: 1) Follow @premiumaidrop on X (Twitter), 2) Join the official Telegram group, and 3) Submit your X username, Telegram username, and a valid EVM wallet address. Only the first 1,000 verified users will qualify.

How much will I receive?

Each qualified user will receive an equal allocation of 20,000 PAP tokens from the total pool of 20,000,000 PAP. The estimated value per allocation is approximately $500 based on the $500,000 total reward pool.

Is this a token sale or presale?

No. This is NOT a token sale, presale, or private sale. PAP Premium Airdrop is exclusively a waitlist rewards program. There are no purchases required. Rewards are distributed fairly to verified early supporters on a first-come, first-served basis.

When will tokens be distributed?

Token distribution details will be announced after the waitlist is filled. All 1,000 verified participants will receive notifications through their submitted social accounts. Stay tuned to our official channels for updates.

What wallets are supported?

Any EVM-compatible wallet is supported. This includes MetaMask, Trust Wallet, Coinbase Wallet, and any other wallet that generates a standard Ethereum address (starting with 0x). Make sure to submit the correct address as it cannot be changed after submission.
